Aspect is a portal for fine art from around the world. It is a tool to foster interest in, create exposure to, and educate the user in both classic and modern art. Aspects goal is to bring a bit of beauty into the day to day by providing a variety of art to a user that they may have never seen before. Often you have a favorite artist or piece of work, those pieces are comfortable and familiar, Aspect looks to expand that.
